A study in ordered and diffusion dithering — the algorithms that allow
continuous-tone images to render on binary output devices.

Implemented several classical algorithms (Floyd–Steinberg, Atkinson, Bayer)
and a few experimental variants. Output includes printable files sized for
thermal and laser printers.
