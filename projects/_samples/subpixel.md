An investigation into subpixel rendering — the technique that uses the
individual RGB elements of each LCD pixel to triple the effective horizontal
resolution for text.

Built a renderer to visualize and compare subpixel, grayscale, and no-hinting
approaches across different typefaces, weights, and sizes.
