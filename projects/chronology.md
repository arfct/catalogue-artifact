An hour hand, cropped to a sliver. Chronology is a Pebble watchface that discards the body of the hand and keeps only its tip — pinned to the current position on the clock's perimeter. The dial stays empty; the rim tells the hour. On a display this small, one moving fragment is enough.

Written in C against the Pebble SDK.
