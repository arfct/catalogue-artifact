The link knows where it wants to go; the browser disagrees. Tap a link to something with a perfectly good native app and Safari opens anyway. Redirect exists for that one stubborn gap: a flexible tool for routing web links into custom URL schemes, so the link lands in the app itself.

The routing is yours to shape — configurable rules, redirect URLs you can hand to anyone, and a companion iOS app for managing the custom schemes. Lives at redirect.app.
